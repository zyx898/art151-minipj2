Nike Dunk Low EMB NBA 75th Anniversary Chicago

Product Description

In celebration of the 75th Anniversary of the NBA, Nike teamed up with the NBA to release a Chicago Bulls-themed Nike Dunk Low.

The Nike Dunk Low EMB 75th Anniversary Chicago features a Sail leather upper with a red toe wrap and black overlays. From there, a commemorative NBA logo woven tongue tags and matching sole completes the design.

The Nike Dunk Low EMB 75th Anniversary Chicago released in October of 2021 and retailed for $110.
